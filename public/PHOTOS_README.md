# Photos

All photos are organized in subfolders:

- /logo/     — Logo files (logo.png also copied to root)
- /hero/     — Hero/banner background photos
- /team/     — Staff and team headshots
- /interior/ — Office, storefront, space photos
- /menu/     — Food, drinks, product photos
- /gallery/  — Additional gallery photos

To add more photos later, drop them into the appropriate subfolder
and tell Claude Code: "Update the site to use the new photos in /public/[folder]/"
